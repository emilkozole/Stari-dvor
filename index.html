Just 48 nautical miles
from the coast of Miami Beach

Just a 20-minute flight from Miami, north of the hustle of Bimini’s Bailey Town, a slender ribbon of land leads to Rockwell Island, a place designed

in every way for discerning travelers seeking a different kind of destination– one defined by tranquility and ease, genuine elegance, and raw natural beauty.

HOW TO GET HERE

With close proximity to the United States, coming over is a breeze.

Sea Plane

Travel by charting a private seaplane from various providers that land directly in North Bimini.

Private Vessel

The most common and practical way to travel is by your private vessel and dock directly behind your home.

Private Jet

Travel on your private jet to Bimini and land on a newly paved and extended 6,400 ft linear runway.

Commercial Flights

Travel to Bimini with Silver Airways in under 30 minutes. Enjoy non-stop flights from Fort Lauderdale to South Bimini.

Ferry

Enjoy the breathtaking views of the horizon and the sparkling turquoise water during this 2-hour journey.

03 - Island Life

Bimini’s Mythological Beauty…


While the deep-sea fishing is nothing short of legendary, the lore of Bimini runs even deeper. “Bimini Road,” a half-mile long series of flat slabs of rock beneath the water’s surface is fabled to be the Lost Steps Of Atlantis, an ancient underwater city of mythological proportions. While the legend of this marine utopia is unverified, the magical energy that exists on Bimini is undeniable. A powerful vibe that pulls travelers from across the globe toward its light.

A peaceful retreat for the cultured and independent-minded traveler.

A secluded slice of paradise where peace and serenity are the ultimate luxury.
